OUR VISION

To assist in the Nigerian economic diversification agenda from oil to non oil foreign exchange earnings by increasing farmers access to international markets, thereby enhancing export development in non-oil sectors generating increased tax revenue leading to direct and indirect employment creation across the whole of the value chain.
