About Hacienda

we are creating opportunities for everyone in the commodities ecosystem

Hacienda Africa Commodity And Exchange Limited. (HACX)  is a limited liability company incorporated by the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. The company is into commodity exchange of products not limited to soybeans, paddy rice, cashew, ginger, maize and sorghum.

HACX, has a robust company and administrative structure that ensures round the clock operational  efficiency added to its trusted and highly experience team with capability of managing people and processes.

The company is proud of reputable captain of industries as advisory board of directors with their experience across wide range of industries. They provide effective board leadership and manage business affairs with all stakeholders by carrying-out their oversight functions which is a key objective for shareholders and the management team.

Based on our analytical skills and understanding of client's required for making an inform decision for strategic choices for investment, our expertise provides the needed opportunity to assess and measure linkages between competing commodity for investment.
Thus, means we assure our clients by addressing their concern to assessing the market size, forecasting demand, pricing and evaluating market drivers is well taking care of and their investment secured with us.

We invest lots of effort in studying information to make us thrive for decades to keep growing and achieve our goals.
